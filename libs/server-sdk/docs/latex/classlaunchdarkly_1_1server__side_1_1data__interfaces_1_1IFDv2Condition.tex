\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A condition observes the orchestrator\textquotesingle{}s stream of synchronizer results and fires when criteria for a synchronizer transition are met.

Each condition plays one of two roles, identified by \mbox{\hyperlink{classlaunchdarkly_1_1server__side_1_1data__interfaces_1_1IFDv2Condition_ac480aff84dbfb08c21137fe9b088e175}{Type()}}\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item k\+Fallback\+: when fired, the orchestrator stops the active synchronizer and starts the next-\/preferred one.
\item k\+Recovery\+: when fired, the orchestrator stops the active fallback synchronizer and returns to the most-\/preferred synchronizer.
\end{DoxyItemize}

Conditions are stateful\+: the orchestrator pushes results into a condition via \mbox{\hyperlink{classlaunchdarkly_1_1server__side_1_1data__interfaces_1_1IFDv2Condition_aee8983da07be4fa3606597084ab7ff06}{Inform()}} so the condition can update its internal state (typically a timer). When the condition\textquotesingle{}s criteria are satisfied, the future returned by \mbox{\hyperlink{classlaunchdarkly_1_1server__side_1_1data__interfaces_1_1IFDv2Condition_a139d04726805a5e4f65c69dc88db3c0c}{Execute()}} resolves with the condition\textquotesingle{}s Type.

Conditions are single-\/use\+: once fired, they are not re-\/armed. The orchestrator builds a fresh condition for each synchronizer activation via an \mbox{\hyperlink{classlaunchdarkly_1_1server__side_1_1data__interfaces_1_1IFDv2ConditionFactory}{IFDv2\+Condition\+Factory}}.

\mbox{\hyperlink{classlaunchdarkly_1_1server__side_1_1data__interfaces_1_1IFDv2Condition_ac87a6ffcd986ca800880fb56c6974795}{Close()}} cancels any pending internal work (e.\+g., a timer) and resolves the future with k\+Cancelled.

Implementations must be thread-\/safe\+: Execute, Inform, Close, and Get\+Type may be called from any thread.
